# Train Connect (Railroad Connect)

A web-based puzzle game where you connect tracks to guide a train and collect items.

## How to Run

1.  Open the folder `railroad_connect`.
2.  Double-click `index.html` to open it in your browser.
    - No server or installation required.

## How to Play

1.  **Start**: Click "START GAME". The train will wait for 5 seconds before moving.
2.  **Place Tracks**: 
    - Drag a track piece from the bottom "Next Track" stock panel and drop it onto the grid.
    - You must connect the tracks to key items (coins) and ensure the train has a continuous path.
3.  **Game Over**: If the train runs off the board or hits a dead end (or empty cell), the game ends.
4.  **Score**: Collect items to increase your score.

## Features

- **Stock System**: You have 10 pieces in your stock. Using one replenishes it randomly.
- **Track Clearing**: Tracks disappear after the train passes over them, adding difficulty.
- **Drag & Drop**: Native drag and drop interface.
- **Responsive Design**: Modern, clean UI.
